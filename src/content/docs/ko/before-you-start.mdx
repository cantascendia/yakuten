## <span style="font-family: var(--font-mono); color: var(--color-accent);">§ 1.1</span> Baseline Lab Work

The following tests should be completed before starting HRT. Most can be ordered through your primary care provider, an informed consent clinic, or services like Planned Parenthood:

## <span style="font-family: var(--font-mono); color: var(--color-danger);">§ 1.2</span> Absolute Contraindications

<ContraindicationBox type="absolute" title="Absolute Contraindications" sectionNum="">

- Active thromboembolic disease (e.g., deep vein thrombosis, pulmonary embolism)
- Confirmed or suspected hormone-sensitive malignancy (e.g., breast cancer, prostate cancer)
- Severe hepatic or renal impairment in acute phase
- Unexplained vaginal bleeding (for transmasculine individuals)
- Known severe allergy to drug components

</ContraindicationBox>

## <span style="font-family: var(--font-mono); color: var(--color-caution);">§ 1.3</span> Relative Contraindications

<ContraindicationBox type="relative" title="Relative Contraindications" sectionNum="">

- Uncontrolled hypertension or cardiovascular disease
- Poorly controlled diabetes mellitus
- Tobacco use (especially age 35+, significantly increases VTE risk)
- History of gallbladder disease
- Migraine with aura (requires careful risk assessment)
- Severe obesity (BMI &gt; 30)

</ContraindicationBox>
