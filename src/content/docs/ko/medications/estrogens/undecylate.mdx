Estradiol undecylate (EU) is one of the longest-acting injectable estradiol esters, with a half-life of approximately 20-30 days, allowing for monthly or longer injection intervals<CitationRef id="kuhl-2005" num={1} authors="Kuhl H" year={2005} title="Pharmacology of estrogens and progestogens" />. Formerly sold as Progynon Depot 100 and other brands, global availability is now very limited. Evidence level is C due to the lack of large-scale HRT clinical trial data.
</DrugInfoBox>

## Pharmacokinetics

Pharmacokinetic profile of estradiol undecylate<CitationRef id="kuhl-2005" num={1} authors="Kuhl H" year={2005} title="Pharmacology of estrogens and progestogens" /><CitationRef id="oriowo-1980" num={2} authors="Oriowo MA et al." year={1980} title="Pharmacokinetics of Estradiol Esters" />:

- **Half-life**: ~20-30 days (longest of all injectable esters)
- **Peak time**: ~3-7 days post-injection
- **Duration of action**: A single injection maintains effective E2 levels for ~30-40 days
- **Steady state**: Due to the very long half-life, requires ~2-3 months to reach steady state

<WarningBox title="Evidence Level C">
Clinical data on estradiol undecylate in transgender HRT is very limited. Most pharmacokinetic data comes from small studies conducted in the 1970s-1980s. When other injectable esters (EV or EC) are available, they are generally recommended as first-line options with stronger evidence.
</WarningBox>

Due to its very long half-life, EU dose adjustments are **slow to take effect**. For situations requiring rapid level adjustment, shorter-acting esters (EV) are more appropriate<CitationRef id="hembree-2017" num={3} authors="Hembree WC et al." year={2017} title="Endocrine Treatment of Gender-Dysphoric/Gender-Incongruent Persons" />.

EU has **extremely limited** global availability. Most users should choose EV or EC as their preferred injectable estradiol.

## Monitoring

- **E2**: Every 3 months, trough draw 1-2 days before injection. Target 100-200 pg/mL
- **T**: Every 3 months. Target &lt;50 ng/dL
- **Liver function (ALT/AST)**: Baseline + every 6 months

Due to the very long half-life, more frequent blood monitoring is needed during initial use to confirm accumulation levels.

<DangerBox title="Safety Limits">
- EU's ultra-long half-life means higher accumulation risk — if side effects occur, the drug will remain in the body for weeks after discontinuation
- Not suitable for situations requiring rapid dose adjustment
- E2 >200 pg/mL does not produce stronger feminization<CitationRef id="hembree-2017" num={3} authors="Hembree WC et al." year={2017} title="Endocrine Treatment of Gender-Dysphoric/Gender-Incongruent Persons" />
- Due to extremely unstable global supply, EU is not recommended as a primary HRT regimen
</DangerBox>
